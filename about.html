Our Foundation

Our Story

Real Elite Contracting was born from a desire to bring a higher standard of service to the home contracting industry. After serving in the military, our founder understood the profound value of discipline, attention to detail, and unwavering integrity. These are not just words to us; they are the bedrock of our business.

We believe that every homeowner deserves a contractor who is reliable, transparent, and committed to excellence. We treat every project, big or small, with the same military precision and dedication that was ingrained in us during our service. We are a family-owned, veteran-operated business, and we are proud to serve our local communities in West Virginia, Maryland, and Virginia.
